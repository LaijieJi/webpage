This was one of those evenings where the light just aligned perfectly. I was walking through the Hofgarten after work when the sunset started painting everything in warm tones.

I pulled out my phone and took a few quick shots — this one ended up being my favourite. The way the light hit the facade of the Bayerische Staatskanzlei made it feel almost surreal.

## Camera settings

- Shot on Canon R10 with RF-S 18-45mm kit lens
