Munich is a city famous by lots of things, either the Oktoberfest, the delicious Weisswursts, for being the home of the great Bayern Munich FC, and many other things, but what striked me the most was the history and the iconic landmarks you can find all around the city. From the stunning Neues Rathaus that welcomes you when you come out of the Marienplatz metro station, to the enormous Frauenkirche, without forgetting the ostentatious Residenz Munchen. All these buildings are majestic.

Taking the chance, I wanted to experiment with my recently acquired camera, so tried to capture the essence of some of these buildings while strolling around the city and admiring the beautiful architecture.

## Camera settings

- Shot on Canon R10 with RF-S 18-45mm kit lens
